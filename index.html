About DynamiX

DynamiX is a game in which you must avoid monster's orbs in order to survive. Use only 4 buttons (WASD) and their configuration to dodge into selected direction.

You'r fast enough. Use the screen border as a teleport to the opposite side. Remember - dead-end situations do not happen. The longer you hold out, the more you get. If you hold out till the end of the music, you will win.

Tip No 1: Use headphones!

Tip No 2: Press f11 to switch into full screen mode.
